About

Andrew Wiggins is a Canadian professional basketball player currently playing for the Golden State Warriors. He was selected first overall in the 2014 NBA draft after playing one year with the Kansas Jayhawks. He will start in the 2022 NBA All-Star Game in Cleveland.

The Wiggins Report is a Canada-based team of basketball fanatics and nerds united in their love for NBA All-Star starter Andrew Wiggins. Our aim is to create a collection of articles, stats, and videos available all in one spot so people can know the truth about Andrew Wiggins: that he could one day be the GCOAT (Greatest Canadian of All Time).
